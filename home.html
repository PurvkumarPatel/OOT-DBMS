DEPARTMENTS

Slideshow Items

  • WATER DEPARTMENT

    Water departments manage the supply and distribution of safe drinking water, oversee wastewater treatment, and ensure compliance with environmental regulations.

  • PUBLIC DEPARTMENT

    Public departments oversee public services such as transportation, education, and housing. .

  • HEALTH DEPARTMENT

    Health department: promotes and protects public health through education, disease surveillance, prevention, and treatment.

  • SAFETY DEPARTMENT

    Safety department: enforces laws, investigates crimes, responds to emergencies, and protects citizens from harm.

  • FOOD DEPARTMENT

    Food department: ensures food safety by inspecting and regulating food establishments, monitoring foodborne illnesses, and enforcing food safety laws.

  • CLEANLINESS DEPARTMENT

    Cleanliness department: maintains public spaces, collects and disposes of waste, and enforces sanitation regulations to prevent public health risks.
